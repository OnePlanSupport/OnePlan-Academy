OnePlan Home Page
In this section:

This module will give an end-to-end dive into the features and functionality of the OnePlan home page, so you, no matter your role, can get an at-a-glance view of your ongoing work.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand what the different Insight types are, and how to manage the Insights that appear on your home page.

  • Collaborate with conversations.

  • Quickly access and manage your plans from your home page.

  • View upcoming or overdue tasks that need your attention.

Watch the OnePlan Home Page Video (5:02)
Home Page Overview

The home page consists of 4 panels: Insights, Conversations, Plans, and Due Soon. These panels are designed to give you a quick look into the status on your ongoing work.

Insights

Insights notify you about plan items that require your attention. The Insights on your home page come from the work items and plans that are assigned to you, so you only get notifications for your own work.

Types of Insights

There are several different kinds of insights. Insight notifications have different levels of severity Less severe insight notifications can be escalated to more severe insights if the issue is not addressed in a set amount of time.

Icon

Insight Type

Meaning

Information Insight

The item is for information only. There are no actions that need to be taken.

Example: A reminder that your timesheet is due tomorrow.

Warning Insight

The item is an issue, but is not urgent. If not addressed, the warning will be escalated to a problem.

Example: A reminder that your timesheet is due today.

Problem Insight

The item is causing issues for the plan and must be addressed.

Example: A notification that your timesheet is overdue.

Compliance Insight

These work items are use in compliance calculations and must be addressed for the plan to remain in compliance.

Example: A plan has not been updated in a specified number of days.

Manage Insights

Click View Details to see all work items that need your attention.

Click  Refresh  to update your Insights in real time. This will pull any updates from relevant plans and tasks into your Home Page, so you know what is going on at all times.

Click Snooze  to remove the Insight from your Insight list. The Insight will reappear the next time Insights are refreshed if it has not been addressed.

Recent Conversations

The conversation threads on your home page come from the work items that are relevant to you, so you can quickly get updates on your work.

Add to the conversation

You can quickly add to a conversation from your home page.

Click View Thread  to see the entire conversation.

Then type in your comment in the field and press Enter. The new comment will appear in the conversation on your home page, and in the plan itself.

Remove comments

To remove a comment from a thread, simply click Remove. Then confirm the removal.

Go to the related work plan

You can jump right to a plan from a comment. Click View Item to go to the related plan.

Recent comments update on the Home Page

Comments made on your work items in the plan will automatically update in the Conversations panel on the home page.

Plans

The Plans panel on the home page allows you to quickly view or search recent plans to you can pick up your work right where you left it.

Due Soon

The Due Soon panel on your home page gives you a quick view of upcoming and overdue tasks.

Overdue tab

The Overdue tab lists all of your tasks that have due dates in the past.

This Week tab

The This Week tab lists all of your tasks that have due dates in the next 7 days.

This Month tab

The This Month tab lists all of your tasks that have due dates in the next 30 days.

Mark tasks complete

You can mark tasks complete by checking the check box next to the desired task.

Access all of your work

You can access all of your work items in My Work (which we will discuss in another course) by clicking the briefcase icon.

Conclusion and Key Takeaways

This module gave you an end-to-end dive into the features and functionality of the OnePlan home page, so you, no matter your role, can get an at-a-glance view of your ongoing work.

The Home Page allows you to:

  • Get quick notifications about ongoing work to keep you in the know.

  • Collaborate with conversations.

  • Quickly access and manage your plans from your home page.

  • View upcoming or overdue tasks that need your attention.
